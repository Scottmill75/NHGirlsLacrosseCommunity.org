Our Mission

Growing the game, together.

NH Girls Lacrosse Community is a shared space for celebrating players, promoting programs, sharing resources, and helping every athlete find a place to learn, belong, and compete.

We believe the strongest future for girls lacrosse begins with collaboration between youth organizations, schools, coaches, clubs, officials, families, and community partners.
