Our Mission

STRONG PLAYERS.
STRONG TEAMS.
STRONGER TOGETHER.

New Hampshire Girls Lacrosse Community is an independent statewide hub created to celebrate athletes, connect families with programs, promote events, and grow the girls’ game.

Every player—from first stick through high school—deserves a place to learn, belong, compete, and lead.
